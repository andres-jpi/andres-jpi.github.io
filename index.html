TYO Genesis

Custom ERP and Organizational Operating Platform

Designed and developed a custom ERP platform for a telecommunications services company operating in Mexico. The platform centralizes information, documents organizational knowledge, standardizes workflows and automates operational processes across project delivery, field operations, procurement and financial workflows.

Through process mapping, workflow automation and data-driven decision-making, TYO Genesis has helped improve productivity by 40–65%, while strengthening collaboration, transparency and operational execution.
