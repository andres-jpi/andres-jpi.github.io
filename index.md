# Andrés Ponce

I am a Mexican systems designer, technologist and entrepreneur working at the intersection of AI, digital infrastructure, creative technology and civic innovation.

My work explores how technology can strengthen organizations, creative communities and civic ecosystems. I design tools, platforms and operational systems that help people collaborate, organize knowledge, automate processes and build more resilient organizations and communities.

---

## Current Work

### Llull Systems

**Co-Founder · Systems Designer**

Llull Systems helps small and medium-sized businesses structure organizational knowledge, standardize operations and implement automation. The company focuses on transforming business processes into scalable operational frameworks through documentation, workflow design, process intelligence and digital tools.

### Kin

**Founder · Product Lead**

Kin is an art infrastructure platform empowering artists, galleries and collectors through digital tools that support collaboration, visibility and sustainable cultural economies. The project is currently in MVP development and local validation.

### Neotrix

**Founder · Product Lead**

Neotrix is a civic technology initiative focused on digital safety, fraud prevention and community resilience in Mexico. The project is developing tools to help people avoid telephone extortion, protect personal digital data and reduce exposure to cyber-enabled fraud.

## Selected Work

### TYO Genesis

**Custom ERP and Organizational Operating Platform**

I designed and developed TYO Genesis, a custom ERP platform for a telecommunications services company operating in Mexico. The platform centralizes information, documents organizational knowledge, standardizes workflows and automates operational processes across project delivery, field operations, procurement and financial workflows.

Through process mapping, workflow automation and data-driven decision-making, TYO Genesis has helped improve productivity by 40–65%, while strengthening collaboration, transparency and operational execution.

### Telecommunications Infrastructure

From 2020 to 2024, I managed engineering projects involving GPON, DWDM, CWDM and LTE technologies, contributing to the deployment and integration of communication infrastructure across Northeast Mexico.

### Creative Technology

My background includes interactive multimedia systems, human-computer interfaces, audiovisual performance, generative music tools, audio-reactive visuals and experimental interfaces. I have worked with Max/MSP, TouchDesigner, Ableton Live, audio plugins, microtonal synthesis and algorithmic sequencing systems.
